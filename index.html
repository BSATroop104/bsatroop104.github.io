Troop 104, Raleigh, NC

Occoneechee Council, North Star District

Welcome to the online home of Troop 104 in Raleigh North Carolina, a proud member of the North Star District of the Occoneechee Council, serving Scouts in Wake, Franklin and Warren counties of North Carolina! We are an active Troop that believes community service, outdoor adventures, and leadership opportunities help provide Scouts the tools they need to grow into successful young adults.

We meet each Monday evening at 7:00 PM at the ELKS LODGE No. 735 5538 Lead Mine Rd, Raleigh, NC 27612 (on the corner of Lead Mine and Millbrook Roads) NOTE: From the first week in July (when we attend Summer Camp) to mid-August, Troop 104 takes a well-deserved summer break from regular Monday meetings. If you are interested in more info on our Troop during that time, please contact the individuals listed at the bottom of this message.

We always welcome guests and new members. We also have a few events planned in the coming months for those interested in scouting.
